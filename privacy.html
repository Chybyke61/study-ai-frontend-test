Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Policy explains how Study.AI collects and uses your data. Your use of the service is also subject to our Terms of Use.

1. Data Collection

We collect account identifiers (email) and content you upload (PDFs/Slides) to provide AI-powered insights. We do not sell this data to third parties.

2. Cookies & Tracking

Study.AI uses essential cookies and local storage to keep you logged in and remember your session preferences. We may use minimal telemetry to track platform performance and error logs to ensure stability across different hardware specifications.

3. AI Processing

Documents are processed via secure APIs. Text chunks are temporarily handled to generate summaries and quizzes. We utilize infrastructure provided by Supabase and Cloudflare to ensure data isolation.

4. Your Rights

You may delete your data at any time via your dashboard. Deleting a document or account removes all associated text indices from our active storage immediately.
